Info.

Currently I develop style of dopasowczaki ("fitty-sheepy"). A painting or a design is composed of multiple fluffy, cute creatures (hence sheepy) that try to fill the emptiness and void that separate them. Their strength and eagerness to fit and connect with each other are so powerful that allow them to overcome entropic generation, and create their own world, that soon becomes a piece of art.
Don't forget that each sheepy has possibility to express their emotions, maybe not as deep and profound as ours (at least at the first glance), but still it's worth listening what they want to whisper to you!
Fitty-sheepy art communicates, that despite of our diversity (color, size, posture, etc.) and current feelings, we can still create something marvelous together, that we are unity, where our past, present and future entangle togheter for better or worse, but still displaying wonders of this life. Despite that all sheepies are static and permament on a convas or in a graphics, they always have different message to convey to you, that is not only customized to your character, but also to how you're feeling today!
